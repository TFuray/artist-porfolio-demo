Jody Bergstresser

Jody's stylized gouache paintings have been shown nationally and internationally. She has completed many public art projects and private commissions (probably the most recognized public work is the Panda done for DCCAH in 2004 which Ben's Chili Bowl owns and displays in the front of their restaurant on U Street NW). 19 paintings are included in DC Commission on the Arts and Humanities Public Art Bank. She is a member of Mid City Artists, Washington DC. Her paintings consists mostly of city scenes with hints of nature, sometimes juxtaposes old buildings with new architecture, uses a wide variety of clean and bright colors and are sketched on site for added uniqueness.
